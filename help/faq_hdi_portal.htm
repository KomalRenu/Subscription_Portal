Portal Management

Topics:
Create a New Subscription Portal
Change a Subscription Portal
Delete a Subscription Portal

Create a New Subscription Portal

Steps

  1. Enter the Portal name in the box.
  2. Click Create New Portal.

Change a Subscription Portal

This changes the Subscription Portal that the end user sees through the Web browser.

Steps

  1. Choose the button next to the portal to make it active.
  2. Click Next.

Delete a Subscription Portal

Steps

  1. Select a portal from the list.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. At the confirmation window click Yes.
